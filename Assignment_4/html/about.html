About ETA Gaming

We are ETA Gaming — a digital hub created by gamers, for gamers. Our mission is to craft immersive, community-driven experiences that unite players across genres and platforms. Whether you're a casual explorer, competitive strategist, or retro pixel lover, ETA Gaming is your next destination.

Founded in 2024 as a student-led initiative, ETA Gaming began with a bold vision: to connect people through play, creativity, and collaboration. From late-night game jams and UI experiments to a growing platform for gamers worldwide, we celebrate the diversity and passion that defines gaming culture.

Our coverage spans everything from new game releases and developer spotlights to industry trends and live gaming events. With a team of dedicated gamers and storytellers, we deliver content that’s insightful, authentic, and built with players in mind.

Whether you’re just starting your gaming journey or deep in the high-score chase, ETA Gaming has something for everyone.

Let's explore the evolving world of gaming, together.

Our Mission

"At ETA Gaming, our mission is to create a vibrant community where gamers can connect, share, and explore the world of gaming together. We strive to provide a platform that celebrates the diversity of gaming experiences and fosters a sense of belonging among players."

Our Vision

"Our vision is to be a leading digital hub for gamers, where innovation meets community. We aim to inspire and empower gamers of all backgrounds to discover new adventures, forge friendships, and express their creativity through play."
